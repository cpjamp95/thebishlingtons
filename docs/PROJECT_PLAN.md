\# 💍 The Bishlingtons Wedding Platform



> \*\*Status:\*\* 🟢 Discovery Phase

> \*\*Current Sprint:\*\* Planning \& Architecture

> \*\*Next Milestone:\*\* Skeleton Application (2 Week Target)



\---



\# ✨ Vision



The Bishlingtons Wedding Platform is more than a wedding website.



It is a modern web application designed to guide guests through every stage of the wedding journey—from receiving their invitation to celebrating the day itself and reliving the memories afterwards.



The platform should feel \*\*professional, personal, effortless and fun\*\*.



\---



\# 🎯 Design Goal



Every feature should make guests think:



> \*\*"Wow... this is incredibly professional, super easy to use and genuinely fun."\*\*



Every design and technical decision should support this goal.



\---



\# 🎯 Product Goals



The platform should:



\* 📅 Provide everything guests need before the wedding.

\* 💌 Allow guests to RSVP online.

\* 🍽 Collect meal choices and dietary requirements.

\* 👤 Give every guest their own personalised profile.

\* 💬 Create a shared community before the wedding.

\* 📢 Keep everyone updated as the wedding approaches.

\* 🎁 Allow guests to contribute to a honeymoon fund or purchase gifts.

\* 📸 Become a shared memory space after the wedding.



\---



\# ✅ Success Criteria



A guest should be able to:



\* Find important information within \*\*30 seconds\*\*.

\* Complete their RSVP in \*\*under 2 minutes\*\*.

\* Return later to edit or review their information.

\* View announcements and updates.

\* Easily access travel, accommodation and wedding details.

\* Feel excited to revisit the platform throughout the wedding journey.



\---



\# 💎 Product Pillars



Every feature should support one or more of these principles.



\## 🎩 Premium

\## ⚡ Effortless

\# 🚫 Out of Scope (Before Invitations)



To maintain focus on the delivery deadline, these features will not be developed before invitations are sent.



\* Private messaging

\* Native mobile applications

\* Multiple visual themes

\* Complex social networking

\* Features that do not improve the guest experience



\---



\# 📌 Development Principles



\* Build the simplest solution that delivers a premium experience.

\* Prioritise guest experience over technical complexity.

\* Mobile-first design.

\* One completed feature at a time.

\* One meaningful Git commit per feature.

\* Keep the platform evolving throughout the wedding journey.
